Privacy Policy

Welcome to Silent Library.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.
This Privacy Policy outlines our data collection practices, your rights regarding your data, and how we protect and use your information.

Data Collection Practices

Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register for a library card, sign up for our newsletter,
or participate in events, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address,
phone number, and mailing address.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, including IP addresses,
browser types, pages visited, and other usage data through cookies and tracking technologies.

Transaction Data: Information about the books you borrow, your reservation history, and any fines or fees incurred.

How We Collect Information

Directly from You:When you fill out forms, register for services, or communicate with us.

Automatically:Through the use of cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies when you visit our website.

From Third Parties: Information may be collected from third-party services integrated with our systems.

Purpose of Data Collection

Service Delivery: To provide library services, process transactions, and communicate with you.

Improvement:To enhance our website, services, and user experience.

Compliance:To comply with legal obligations and protect the rights and safety of our users and the library.

User Rights

Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request corrections to any inaccuracies.
To do so, please contact us using the information on our contact page.

Deletion and Opt-Out

You may request the deletion of your personal information or opt out of certain data collection practices.
However, some information may be retained for legal or operational purposes.

Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw your consent to the use of your personal information at any time.
However, this will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

Security Measures

Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls.

Incident Response

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law and take steps to mitigate any potential harm.

Cookies and Tracking

Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

Types of Cookies
  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how you use our website to improve its performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your browsing habits to show you relevant advertisements.
Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date.


Thank you for trusting Silent Library with your information.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for all our users.
