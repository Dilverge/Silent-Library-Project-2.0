Silent Library's Monthly Book Club

A Community Initiative for interested participants to gather and discuss their favourite books! Every month, a book is selected from the Library's collection, available for everyone to borrow and read. Join us to discover new titles or rekindle your love for past reads!

The Courage to be Disliked

Author: Ichiro Kishimi and Fumitake Koga
Category: Self Help
Status: Unavailable

Description
A single book can change your life. Already an enormous bestseller in Asia, with more than 3 million copies sold, The Courage to be Disliked demonstrates how to unlock the power within yourself to be the person you truly want to be.

The Girl Who Survived

Author: Ellie Midwood
Category: Fiction
Status: Available

Description
Germany, 1941: "We live together, or we die together." A novel that will stay with you forever, The Girl Who Survived tells the inspiring true story of Ilse Stein, a German Jew who was imprisoned in a ghetto--and who fell in love with the man she was supposed to loathe.
